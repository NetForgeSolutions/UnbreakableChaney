✦ What We Do

Programs built on

Every program we run was shaped by the people it serves. We don't impose solutions — we co-create them alongside community members who know their needs best. 💜

01
🧠
Trauma Recovery & Mental Health

Trauma-informed counseling, support groups, and mental health education for individuals and families navigating the effects of adverse experiences. We provide a safe, culturally affirming space to process, heal, and grow.

Ongoing
02
🌟
Youth Mentorship & Development

Structured mentorship pairing youth ages 10–18 with trained adult mentors from the community. Programming includes academic support, life skills, career exploration, and leadership development.

Ages 10–18
03
💼
Economic Empowerment

Financial literacy workshops, job readiness training, résumé building, and connections to employment resources. We believe economic stability is a cornerstone of individual and community health.

Open Enrollment
04
🏘️
Family Stability & Support

Holistic support services for families including crisis navigation, housing assistance referrals, parenting education, and case management — keeping families intact and on the path toward stability.

Family-Centered
05
📚
Community Education & Advocacy

Workshops, community conversations, and advocacy efforts that build awareness around trauma, systemic inequity, and the pathways to healing. Knowledge is power — and we share it freely.

Public Events
06
🤲
Peer Support Network

A trained peer support network connecting community members with lived experience to those who are just beginning their healing journey. Because sometimes you need someone who truly gets it.
