Our Story 💜

Born from experience.
Built for community. 🌈

Unbreakable Chaney was founded with a deep understanding of what it means to live through trauma — and the fierce belief that healing is possible when community shows up. Our organization was built not in boardrooms, but in living rooms, church halls, and community centers.


We have spent years listening, learning, and leading. Our team brings lived experience alongside professional expertise, because we know that both matter when you are trying to build something real. ✨

Mission 🎯

Our Mission

To break cycles of trauma and build resilient, healthy communities — for every person, in every neighborhood. 🌈

Vision 🌟

Our Vision

A world where every person has the support to live a whole and healthy life — filled with joy, connection, and possibility. ✨

Approach 💡

How We Work

Community-led, trauma-informed, evidence-based, and rooted in radical compassion. We follow the community's lead — always. 💜

Core Values ⭐

What guides everything we do. 💫

🌱
Healing First

Every decision, program, and partnership is filtered through one question: does this support healing? We lead with compassion and meet people exactly where they are.

🤝
Community as Power

We believe communities already hold the solutions. Our role is to amplify, resource, and stand alongside the brilliance that already exists in our neighborhoods.

🔓
Radical Transparency

We are accountable to the people we serve. That means honest communication, open books, and willingness to learn when we fall short.

⚡
Relentless Hope

Change is possible — always. We approach the hardest circumstances with stubborn optimism and unwavering commitment to what could be.
